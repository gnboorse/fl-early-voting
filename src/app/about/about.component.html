About

This site serves as a resource for those looking for information about early voting locations within the state of Florida. The Florida Department of State's Division of Elections does post this data on their site, but it is in a format that is confusing and inaccessible for most users. This site was created to provide Florida residents with easily-accessible information about early voting polling places and the hours that each location is open.
